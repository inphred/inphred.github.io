Light sources for previously inaccessible wavelengths

Our nanoporous platform enables two families of products — manufacturable at foundry scale — covering wavelengths that have eluded the industry for 30 years.

InP · 1,300 – 2,300 nm
Product 01

SWIR VCSEL

Short-wave infrared vertical-cavity surface-emitting laser — the world's first manufacturable SWIR VCSEL using a homoepitaxial nanoporous InP DBR. Eye-safe, high signal-to-noise, and compatible with existing compound semiconductor foundries.

  • Eye-safe wavelengths (1,300–2,300 nm) enabling consumer-facing AR/VR and LiDAR applications
  • ~3–12× SNR improvement over NIR (850 nm) with ~1.5–4× range extension
  • ~30% power conversion efficiency (PCE) demonstrated; industry reliability standards met
  • Wafer-level fabrication and on-wafer testing — compatible with mature InP telecom foundries
  • Custom solutions available: emitter + ASIC integration, module-level design

SWIR VCSEL · InP material system

Why now? The industry has pursued InP VCSELs for 30 years. InPHRED's nanoporous DBR eliminates the fundamental barrier — insufficient refractive-index contrast in homoepitaxial InP — delivering a manufacturable solution for the first time.

Visible RC-LED · GaN material system

Resonant-cavity advantage. By placing the LED active region inside a resonant optical cavity, emission is directed, spectrally narrow, and significantly brighter than a standard LED — unlocking applications that broad LEDs can't serve.

GaN · 450 – 570 nm
Product 02

Visible RC-LED

A resonant-cavity LED operating across the visible blue-to-green spectrum (450–570 nm). High spectral purity and strong directionality make this a superior sensor for health wearables and a competitive emitter for ultra-short-reach optical interconnects.

  • High spectral purity — narrow emission linewidth versus standard broadband LEDs
  • Strong directionality and radiance — more signal on target, less ambient noise
  • Improved accuracy for heart rate, SpO₂, and novel biomarkers (anti-oxidants, carotenoids) across all skin tones
  • Suitable for ultra-short-reach (USR) chiplet and die-to-die optical interconnects
  • GaN nanoporous DBR process — same patented platform as InP VCSEL
