Frequently asked

Do you offer sample devices for evaluation?

Yes. We supply SWIR VCSEL and visible RC-LED samples to qualified OEM and system integration partners. Evaluations are conducted under NDA. Please use the contact form to initiate a discussion.

What wavelengths are available today?

Our primary SWIR VCSEL offering centres on the 1,300–1,700 nm range using mature telecom InP epi. Extended wavelengths up to 2,200 nm are in development. Visible RC-LEDs are available across 450–570 nm.

Can you support custom wavelength or form-factor requirements?

Yes. Our platform is highly configurable for specific wavelength targets, light modes, and more. We welcome custom engagement discussions for OEM applications requiring tailored performance.

Are you seeking investment?

We are actively seeking $20M+ in Series B follow-on capital to pursue three market segments in parallel: consumer sensing, digital health, and data center optical interconnects. Please reach out via the contact form.

Where are you based, and do you ship internationally?

We operate across the US and Taiwan with around-the-clock coverage. We work with international OEM partners — please contact us to discuss logistics for your geography.

Do you collaborate with universities or research labs?

Yes. As a university spin-out startup, InPHRED has deep academic roots. We are open to joint research, sponsored programmes, and technology licensing discussions with academic institutions.
