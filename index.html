Fabless business model

From foundry epi to volume production

End-to-end process flow (simplified)

Mature foundry epi
Homoepitaxially grown InP or GaN where alternating n⁺ and undoped layers form the n-DBR
→

Electrochemical etch
Selective conversion of n⁺ layers to nanoporous (NP) semiconductor; undoped layers unchanged
→

Tunable index stack
Porosity controls Δn; DBR reflectivity can be tuned from 60% (RC-LED) to >99.5% (VCSEL)
→

Mature device fab
Standard wafer-level process; on-wafer testing; existing semiconductor foundry equipment
→

Volume production
High yield, commercial cost structure — the only approach achieving this in both InP and GaN
